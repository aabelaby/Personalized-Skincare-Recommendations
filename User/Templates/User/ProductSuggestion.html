Disclaimer: How to Determine Your Skin Type

The following steps are provided as a general guide to help you identify your skin type. This is not a substitute for professional dermatological advice. Results may vary, and we recommend consulting a skincare professional for an accurate assessment.

  1. Clean Your Face: Wash your face with a gentle cleanser and lukewarm water. Pat dry with a clean towel. Avoid harsh scrubbing or hot water, as this may affect the outcome.
  2. Wait 1 Hour: Allow your skin to rest for one hour without applying any products or touching your face. This helps your skin return to its natural state.
  3. Check for Oil: After one hour, observe your face in a mirror or gently press a tissue against your skin. Note areas that appear oily, shiny, or dry.
  4. Identify Your Skin Type:
    • Dry: Skin feels tight, flaky, or rough with no visible oil.
    • Combination: Oily on the forehead, nose, and chin (T-zone), but dry or normal on the cheeks.
    • Oily: Skin appears shiny or greasy across most areas.
  5. Visual Reference: Refer to the accompanying image to help match your observations to a skin type. For best results, repeat this process over a few days if you’re uncertain.

Note: Skin type can change due to factors like weather, hormones, or product use. This method is a starting point and may not fully capture your skin’s needs. Proceed at your own discretion. Ready to explore your skincare options?
